GREAT WAS THE BATTLE THAT FOLLOWED, IN WHICH THE KAURAVAS WERE TOTALLY ANNIHILATED. THE INCIDENTS OF THE KURUKSHETRA BATTLE ARE RECOUNTED IN ALL THEIR GLORIOUS DETAIL TO THIS DAY. BUT MORE MEMORABLE STILL WAS THE DISCOURSE THAT LORD KRISHNA GAVE TO ARJUNA, WHICH HAS INSTRUCTED, ENLIGHTENED AND INSPIRED INDIANS DOWN THE AGES.
