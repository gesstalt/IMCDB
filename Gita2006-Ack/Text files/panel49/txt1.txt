THE KAURAVA ARMY HAD ELEVEN DIVISIONS AND WAS THUS NUMERICALLY FAR SUPERIOR TO THE PANDAVA ARMY. TO WITHSTAND THE ENEMY ONSLAUGHT, THE PANDAVA ARMY HAD BEEN ARRIVED IN NEEEDLE-LIKE FORMATION. ARJUNA WAS IN THE CENTRE IN HIS GHARIOT, TO WHICH HAD BEEN VOKED FOUR WHITE HORSES. KRISHNA WAS HIS CHARIOTEER.
