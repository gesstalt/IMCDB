AFTER HIS MOETHER'S DEATH, SHANKARA TRAVELLED TWICE MORE THROUGHOUT INDIA ON THE FIRST OF THESE TRIPS, HE ESTABLISHED THE MATHS AT DWARKA IN THE WEST WITH HASTAMLAKA AS ITS HEAD, AND PURI IN THE EAST WITH PADMAPADA AS THE HEAD SPRIRTUAL CENTERES AND TEMPLES ESTABLISHED BY HIM ALSO FLOURISH AT KANCHI PASHUPATINATH AND OTHER CITIES.
